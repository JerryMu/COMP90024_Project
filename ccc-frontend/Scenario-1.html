Senario 1:

The pandemic in Australia is being well controlled, however,  it continues to impact many other countries in the world.  The primary task recently is to speed up vaccination in Australia. Above all, we want to know people's attention on Covid-19 by searching the amount of coronavirus related tweets - those tweets include relative topics within text - in different cities. And then comparing people's attention with the real Covid-19 situation in different areas to find out if any relation exists.
