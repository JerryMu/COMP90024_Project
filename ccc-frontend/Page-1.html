Scenario 4:
The impact of Covid-19 on the labour market.

Scenario Description:

The coronavirus affects all walks of life. During 2020, many people started to work at home. Due to the lockdown, plenty of companies are suffering from a hard time. As the company layoffs and bankruptcy increase, we want to investigate how the labour market changed before and after the pandemic. In this section, we are using labour market summary data collected at the end of 2018 and 2020 to make the comparison.
